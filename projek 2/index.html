Parts of Speech

1. Noun

A word that represents a person, place, thing, or idea.

Example: Software, Engineer, Computer, City.

2. Verb

An action or state of being.

Example: Develop, Code, Create, Is, Are.

3. Adjective

A word that describes a noun.

Example: Modern, Clean, Interactive, Fast.
×

Essential Tenses

1. Simple Present Tense

Digunakan untuk kebiasaan, fakta, atau kejadian yang terjadi saat ini.

Example: I code every day. The sun rises in the east.

2. Simple Past Tense

Digunakan untuk kejadian yang sudah selesai di masa lalu.

Example: I finished my project yesterday. She went to the campus.

3. Simple Future Tense

Digunakan untuk kejadian yang akan terjadi di masa depan.

Example: I will learn React JS tomorrow. We are going to build an app.

Daily Expressions

1. Greetings (Salam)

Cara menyapa orang lain dalam situasi formal maupun kasual.

Example: Good morning! / Hello, how are you doing? / What's up?

2. Introducing Yourself (Memperkenalkan Diri)

Kalimat dasar untuk memperkenalkan diri kepada orang baru.

Example: Hi, my name is [Name]. I am a software engineering student.

3. Asking for Help (Meminta Bantuan)

Cara sopan untuk meminta tolong dalam bahasa Inggris.

Example: Could you please help me with this code? / Do you mind explaining this to me?
